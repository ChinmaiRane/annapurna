The अन्नपूर्णा's Vision

The Attitude of "Feed to Feel"

Children are a country's future. For a developing country like India, the growth of poor children is critical to the country's progress, and their education is the foundation of that success. However, children's education cannot be achieved without ensuring the family's well-being – a child can attend school on a regular basis only if the family, particularly the mother, is healthy and empowered; the child receives adequate nutrition; and the family has decent livelihood opportunities and a steady income. Recognizing this, Annapurna has created a lifelong approach that covers the needs of children, their families, and the greater community, beginning in the corridors of education. Annapurna is convinced that permanent change will not occur unless civil society members are actively involved in the development process. Annapurna uses this Civic Driven Change strategy to educate and involve the civil society, making it an active participant in all of its humanitarian activities.
