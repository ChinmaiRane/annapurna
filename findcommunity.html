The NESH community fridge project

Nobody Ever Sleeps Hungry ( NESH ), a registered NGO, has installed more than 40 community fridges in Mumbai – and there is one near you !! A community fridge is a dignified and continuous way of helping the poor. You can cook an extra meal at home or if you have leftovers that could be packed properly and kept in the fridge so that whoever is in need of food can take it. The fridge judges no one. The needy can pick up the food from the fridge.. It could be ready made food or even fruits / vegetables. So dear Mumbaikar, what are you waiting for?? Together let’s make a difference so that Nobody Ever Sleep Hungry ! Find the nearest Fridge location!
